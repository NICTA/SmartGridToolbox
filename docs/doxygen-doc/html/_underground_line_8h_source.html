22  /// @brief An underground power line.
23  ///
24  /// As in Gridlab-D, we take direction from Kersting: Distribution System Modelling and Analysis.
25  ///
26  /// Phases (e.g. A, B, and C) are individually surrounded by *either* a concentric stranded neutral, or a neutral
27  /// tape shield. In addition, there may be a separate neutral wire, which does not have its own shielding as it is
28  /// not expected to carry (much) current. Thus, for three phase, there are either 6 or 7 conductors to consider,
29  /// depending on whether there is a separate neutral wire. Conductors 1-3 and 7 are the usual type of conductor
30  /// with their own particular GMR specified, etc. Conductors 4-6 represent either the concentric neutrals or the
31  /// tape.
