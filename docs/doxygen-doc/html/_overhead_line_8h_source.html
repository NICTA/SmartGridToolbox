22  /// @brief An overhead power line.
23  ///
24  /// An overhead line with nCond conductors, with nPhase of the conductors corresponding to
25  /// explicitly modelled phases (possibly including an explicit neutral phase), and the remaining
26  /// (nCond - nPhase) conductors being grounded neutral wires that influence the inductance of the line
27  /// but are not explicitly modelled as phases in the network. The conductors themselves may (or may not)
28  /// consist of bundles of two, three or four single wires, usually separated by a small air gap.
29  /// For bundled wires, a bundling distance is defined which is the distance between the wires
30  /// for two or three bundle wires (assumed to be in equilateral triangle in the latter case), or
31  /// the side length of a square configuration for four bundled wires.
32  /// As in Gridlab-D, we take direction from Kersting: Distribution System Modelling and Analysis.
33  /// @ingroup PowerFlowCore
